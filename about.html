LAYREX

Layarex, a registered Limited Liability Partnership (LLP), stands as a versatile and innovative company housing two distinctive ventures: Packup Cover Factory and 78 Media. At the forefront, Layarex specializes in providing a range of comprehensive printing solutions that cater to diverse needs. Packup Cover Factory, one of its prime subsidiaries, operates as a pioneering force in the realm of cover and paper box printing. Renowned for its creativity and precision, Packup Cover Factory transforms ordinary packaging into artistic expressions, delivering exceptional printed materials that leave a lasting impact.

78 Media, another significant wing under the Layarex umbrella, operates as an offset printing press with a remarkable portfolio. Specializing in a wide array of products including invitation cards, business cards, e-posters, and flex printing, 78 Media harnesses the power of traditional offset printing to create tangible pieces of art that captivate audiences. With an unwavering commitment to quality and an adept understanding of design nuances, 78 Media ensures that every piece that leaves their press is a testament to their dedication to excellence.

Together, Layarex, Packup Cover Factory, and 78 Media form an integrated entity that thrives on innovation, precision, and artistic finesse. Their collective contributions to the printing industry have solidified their reputation as a go-to destination for individuals and businesses seeking top-tier printing solutions that go beyond the ordinary.

78 MEDIA

78 Media stands as a distinguished name in the realm of printing, offering a comprehensive array of services that cater to diverse printing needs.
